Last class - Thoughts on Web Programming...

The "needs improvment" section

  • Needs more diversity - more female and minority groups.
  • Cowboys & Ego.

Things to avoid

  • Avoid building a Conten Management Systems (CMS) from scratch.
  • Avoid people who build CMS's from scratch.
  • Delete/throw away job ads with the words ['ninja','guru','rockstar'].
  • Avoid working at a place where you are the smartest programmer.
  • Don't sit too much, get a standing desk.

The good parts

  • You won't meet too many unemployed programmers.
  • Pick your own path.
  • Telecommuting was probably created by a programmer.
  • "Waiting for permission" isn't always required on the Internet.
